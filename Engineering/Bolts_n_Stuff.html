Follow these steps to get live prices from your server into the calculator.

  1. Copy the Shopping List: Click the "Copy Auctionator Shopping List" button above.
  2. Import to Auctionator: In-game, open the Auction House, go to the "Shopping" tab, click "Import," and paste the list.
  3. Scan and Export: Select the new shopping list and let it scan. Once done, click "Export Results" and copy the text that appears.
  4. Import to Calculator: Paste the copied text into the "Import Prices" box below and click the "Import Prices" button. The calculator will update automatically.
